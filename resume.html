About Me

I am passionate about developing secure and reliable software solutions that are a delight to use. At L3Harris, I help develop image rectification tools used by 100+ company employees to process and analyze satellite imagery. Outside work, I'm building a machine learning model to classify images and expanding a web application to test student code submissions for security vulnerabilities. I am proficient in Java, C++, Python, SQL, and JavaScript and have 5+ years of experience working with defense contractors, government agencies, and national security think tanks. With a masters degree in Security Studies, I combine a strategic perspective, excellent critical thinking skills, and technical expertise to deliver results to challenging problems. I currently reside in Alexandria, Virginia and will move to Madrid, Spain in July 2023. In my free time, I enjoy hiking, skiing, and studying mathematics.
