About Me

I am passionate about developing secure and reliable software solutions that are a delight to use. At L3Harris, I helped develop image rectification tools used by 100+ company employees to process and analyze satellite imagery. I now live with my active duty U.S. Army spouse in Madrid, Spain, where I am expanding my skills in full stack web development and cybersecurity. I recently earned my GIAC Certified Incident Handler (GCIH) certification after passing the GCIH exam with a 99%. I am proficient in Java, C++, Python, SQL, and JavaScript and have 5+ years of experience working with defense contractors, government agencies, and national security think tanks. With a masters degree in Security Studies, I combine a strategic perspective, excellent critical thinking skills, and technical expertise to deliver results to challenging problems. In my free time, I enjoy hiking, skiing, kickboxing, studying mathematics, and exploring Madrid.
